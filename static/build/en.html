What we offer

  • Water-based cleaning
  • Dry cleaning
  • Cleaning bags and shoes
Water-based cleaning

The most ecological and modern solution. We clean your items with water and safe cleaning materials. Your clothes are washed with care and aren’t exposed to powerful chemical agents.

We use this method to clean: shirts, linen, jeans, and other items made from materials which aren’t afraid of water.

Dry cleaning

Cleaning for the toughest cases. With this method we don’t use water, only special cleaning materials that can handle everything thrown at it. Clothes you thought you would have to say goodbye to forever come back fresh, clean and like-new.

We use this method to clean: absolutely everything you might despair over.

Cleaning bags and shoes

Washing your favorite leather jacket or fur coat at home isn’t the best idea. Leave it to us.

At Dry Harder, our specialists clean your valuable items, including bags and shoes, expertly and with care. Everything will look like new again.

Our principles

О Dry Harder

We love good-looking clothes, we value good service, and we always disliked mundane chores. That’s why we started Dry Harder.

We work diligently so that you are happy with your clothes

We carefully tend to your clothes and combine handwashing and cleaning in state-of-the-art Italian and German machines.

The most state-of-the-art cleaning solutions are selected for each individual item depending on the type of fabric and soiling.

Neatly pressing and folding clothes is just as important as washing them well. That’s why we use only professional presses and biodegradable plastics and carefully listen to your special requests.

We don’t want you to waste your time.
We think of everything. With our online accounts, you can pay for your order instantly. We offer a simple way to pay, instant drop-off of clothes, and fast pick-up. After all, no one likes to spend their time at the dry-cleaner’s. If you have any special requests, we’d be happy to hear them. Maybe you want us to sort clothes by color? Or fold them for your suitcase? Or maybe you just want something to be pressed? Just let us know.

Process

Assessment 1

Before washing, every item is carefully examined. We know it’s not nice when buttons break or trimming gets ruined. That’s why we check your clothes and reinforce everything before cleaning.

Hand cleaning 2

Before your clothes are washed, they are cleaned by hand. This ensures the highest quality and means that your clothes will serve you longer.

Machine cleaning 3

After they have been cleaned your clothes by hand, our experts decide the best machine for them. We use only state-of-the-art Italian and German machines and chose the safest method of cleaning your clothes.

Pressing 4

Every item needs to be cared for after washing. With the help of our professional equipment, we make sure your clothes are wrinkle-free.

Packing 5

Do you have any request for how your clothes should be packaged? Just let us know. Do you go on business trips often? We can fold your clothes so they can go right in your suitcase. Do you want us to sort your clothes by color? Or separate men’s and women’s? It’s no problem.
